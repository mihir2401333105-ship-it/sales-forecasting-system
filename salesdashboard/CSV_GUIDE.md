# CSV Data Format Guide

To get accurate AI forecasts, your CSV file MUST follow this format exactly:

### Required Headers:
`Year,Month,Sales`

### Requirements:
1. **Year**: 4-digit number (e.g., 2023, 2024).
2. **Month**: Number from 1 to 12.
3. **Sales**: Numeric value representing your sales performance.

### Tips
- Include at least 2 years of historical data for best results.
- Ensure there are no gaps in monthly reporting.
- Use `sample_sales_template.csv` to get started.
